Academic Experience

I was a research/teaching assistant, in the Department of Physics, Istanbul Technical University, September 2000 and also was a Laboratory assistant for the Physics 101, 102, 106 courses. (in English) . After I finished my PhD, I was appointed as a Asst. Prof. Dr. in the Department of Physics, Harran University by the Council of Higher Education. I served as a lecturer (Waves and Oscillations, Thermodynamics), in the Department of Physics, Harran University, 2008-2013. Furthermore, I served as a lecturer (Physics I and Physics II), in Department of Mathematics and Chemistry, Harran University, 2008-2013. I was a Visiting Scholar at the Department of Physics, Oklahama State University, Stillwater, Oklahama, USA in 2011 under supervisor Prof. Dr. Girish S. Agarwal. I was appointed as Assoc. Prof. Dr. in Education Faculty, Dumlupinar University between 2013-2017.


I was serving as an Assoc. Prof. Dr. in Dumlupinar University, Kutahya, Turkey, when on Jully 15, 2016 there was an attempted coup in Turkey. Although I did nothing illegal, I was dismissed without trial by a “decree with the force of law”by the Turkish government on January 6, 2017, together with 131,922 other government employees. So far only a handful (17,265) have been reinstated.
